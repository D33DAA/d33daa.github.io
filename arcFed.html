UAC STRIKE FLEET ATTACKS DELTA CORP OFFICES

3202-11-04

The planet of Teus (Actoylm, 0403) was rudely awoken in the early hours this morning to the sound of UAC strike craft entering the atmosphere in an attempt to destroy the local offices of Delta Corporation, and harm their civilian workers. Rumours speculate the UAC targeted the lives of Delta Corporation employees in response to Delta's increasing market share in their local economy. Miraculously, the builidngs and people remained unscathed (not counting the legal damage done by UAC Lawyers mere days before the incident) due to what witnesses speculate to be a large scale weapons malfunction on the UAC fleet. The CEO of Delta Corporation released this statement after the attack:

"We are horrified to see DeltaCorp employees attacked and detained by UAC military personal. This is tyrannical behaviour that we are surprised to see by a previously peaceful collective."

COBALT RUSH IN PIRATE SYSTEMS

3202-10-14

ArcNet probes and passing grey-law ships are reporting that a recent discovery of large cobalt deposits in the asteroid belt of the Romny system (0604) have lead to a major increase in the amount of mining operations in the area. The system, home to infamous pirate organization The Maw, has a major illegal spaceport, through which most of these mining operations have been setup. Increased numbers of unlicensed miners have been reported in the nearby area, and new industries are being hastily organised. This new rush may be a break in the recent shortages of Cobalt in the sector, however, ArcNet feels responsible to warn against trade with The Maw, due to their illicit activities.

New constructions point to Delta Corporation aiming to expand its production, by procuring more raw materials.
